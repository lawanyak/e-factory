ABOUT US
E-FACTORY is a website for vendors and bakery owners. This website enables them to a direct interaction. And it also helps to know the original manufacturing cost and saving the time of both the vendors and users. In this website the factory owner and the bakery runners will be registered their details.

The users(bakery runners) will know the vendors and their products. If they want those product, can purchase it in online. And we will maintain all the details of the products of the vendors.

Through this website the vendors will know their customers. Here there is no agent or mediator between the vendors and users.Also the user will know the products of each factory and cost of the product.
